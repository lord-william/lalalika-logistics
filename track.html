Tracking FAQ

How often is tracking information updated?

Our tracking system updates every 30 seconds when your package is in transit, providing you with the most current location and status information.

What if my tracking number isn't working?

If your tracking number isn't found, please wait 24 hours after shipping as it may take time to appear in our system. If the issue persists, contact our support team.

Can I track multiple packages at once?

Currently, you can track one package at a time. For multiple packages, please enter each tracking number separately or contact our support for bulk tracking options.

Do you send tracking notifications?

Yes! We send SMS and email notifications for major status updates including pickup, transit milestones, out for delivery, and successful delivery.
